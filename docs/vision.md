# CaliAI – Vision & Product Definition

## 1. Overview

CaliAI is an AI-powered calisthenics coaching platform that provides intelligent form analysis and personalized feedback using computer vision.

The goal is to make professional-level coaching accessible to anyone with a camera.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Calisthenics athletes often train without professional supervision.

Common issues:

- Incorrect form
- Poor technique progression
- Increased injury risk
- Slow skill acquisition

Most users rely on:

- YouTube videos
- Trial and error
- Generic workout plans

There is no personalized, objective, AI-driven form feedback system tailored specifically for calisthenics.

---

## 3. Target Users

- Beginner calisthenics athletes
- Intermediate athletes learning advanced skills
- Self-trained individuals without access to coaches
- Athletes who want measurable progress tracking

---

## 4. Core Value Proposition

CaliAI provides:

- Video-based form analysis
- AI-powered posture & movement feedback
- Progress tracking over time
- Skill-specific recommendations

Unlike generic fitness apps, CaliAI focuses specifically on:

- Bodyweight training
- Static holds
- Dynamic calisthenics skills
- Form precision

---

## 5. Core Features (MVP)

1. User Authentication
2. Workout Session Logging
3. Video Upload
4. AI Form Analysis
5. Feedback Report:
   - Form score
   - Identified mistakes
   - Suggested corrections
   - Progress comparison

---

## 6. Long-Term Vision

Future expansions may include:

- Real-time feedback mode
- Personalized training programs
- Skill progression engine
- Community leaderboard
- Injury risk detection

---

## 7. Product Philosophy

CaliAI is designed to:

- Be data-driven
- Provide actionable feedback (not generic advice)
- Encourage measurable progress
- Feel like a personal coach, not just a tracker
